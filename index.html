About

Okoye serves the Black Panther as a personal bodyguard and the nation of Wakanda as a torchbearer of tradition and virtue. Okoye is as deadly as she is wise, and stands as one of T’Challa’s most trusted allies and companions.

Powers and Abilities

  • An amazingly well-trained and skilled fighter
  • Okoye is capable of besting notably formidable foes, including numerous combatants at once.
  • Okoye is able to hit distant targets with deadly precision, throwing her spear and hitting intended targets with fantastic accuracy.
